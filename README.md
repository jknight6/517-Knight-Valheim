# 517-Knight-Valheim
This repository is to talk about the game _Valheim_ and some of my favorite things about it.

## About _Valheim_

[_Valheim_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lf2xG7R0fxk&pp=ygUHdmFsaGVpbQ%3D%3D) is an exploration and survival game set in a procedurally-generated world inspired by Viking mythology. Players gather resources, craft gear, build bases, and kill monsters to prove they are worthy of Valhalla.

There are seven primary biomes in the game currently, with one more being added before the game's full release:

- Meadows
- Black Forest
- Swamp
- Mountains
- Plains
- Mistlands
- Ashlands
- Deep North (coming soon)

There is also an Ocean biome that can be traversed by ship, but it is not the same as the other biomes since it does not have a boss (known as Forsaken) to defeat.

## Biomes

Here is a rundown of the biomes in _Valheim_.

### Meadows

![Meadows](https://i.redd.it/5tqamp648mjc1.png)

The starting point for every new world, the Meadows biome is a relatively safe and resource-rich area perfect for building your first base. It is characterized by green, rolling hills, rivers, and forests. This is where you'll find early-game resources like wood, stone, and wild raspberries. While relatively peaceful, you may encounter low-level threats like Greylings, boars, and the first boss, Eikthyr. 

### Black Forest

![Black Forest](https://aywren.com/2021/pic/20210214211438_1.jpg)

The Black Forest is a dense and ominous coniferous forest. It is home to new and more dangerous enemies, including Greydwarfs, skeletons guarding burial chambers, and trolls. This is a critical biome for progression, as it's where you'll mine copper and tin to craft bronze gear. You'll also explore burial chambers to find valuable Surtling Cores, which are vital for crafting a smelter and kiln.

### Swamp

![Swamp](https://www.nme.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Valheim-root-armour-2000x1270-1.jpg)

The Swamp is a treacherous, waterlogged, and perpetually rainy biome where your stamina is constantly drained. Navigating the muddy terrain is difficult, and the perpetual dim lighting and thick trees make it easy to get lost. The Swamp is fraught with dangers, including poisonous blobs and leeches, as well as undead Draugr and Wraiths. Its primary draw is the Sunken Crypts, which contain valuable Scrap Iron for crafting your first set of high-tier armor and weapons.

### Mountains

![Mountains](https://static1.thegamerimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Valheim-Mountain-biome-area-guide-resources-preparation-beasts-threats-creatures-drops-techradar.jpg)

The Mountains are cold, treacherous, high-altitude biomes where you'll need frost resistance to survive. These peaks are home to wolves, drakes, and formidable stone golems. However, the harsh conditions yield a valuable reward: veins of silver ore, which can be found with the help of a Wishbone. Exploring the frigid landscape can also lead to hidden Frost Caves and the biome's boss, Moder.

### Plains

![Plains](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcSpQR1zeIA2OFYsPZ7Ep13GKZ28z_ufmjcRlw&s)

Deceptively sunny and flat, the Plains biome is a major difficulty spike, populated by incredibly dangerous creatures. The biome is characterized by golden grass, mesas, and dangerous wildlife, including the agile and aggressive Fuling tribes, the tanky Lox, and the infamous, nearly-invisible Deathsquitos. The Plains offer important late-game materials like black metal scrap, flax, and barley. The final boss of the original release, Yagluth, resides in this biome.

### Mistlands

![Mistlands](https://www.godisageek.com/wp-content/uploads/Valheim-Mistlands-News.jpg)

The Mistlands is an eerie, fog-covered biome filled with giant, ancient tree roots and unusual rock formations. The pervasive mist makes navigation and combat extremely challenging until you've acquired a Wisplight. Powerful and unique enemies, like the insect-like Seekers and fire-breathing Gjalls, reside here. The Mistlands also introduce new magical mechanics and offer Black Marble and other resources found within Infested Mines.

### Ashlands

![Ashlands](https://assetsio.gnwcdn.com/Untitled_BVI2ptD.png?width=1600&height=900&fit=crop&quality=100&format=png&enable=upscale&auto=webp)

Located at the southern edge of the world, the Ashlands is a volcanic wasteland of fire and ash. Reaching this biome is a feat in itself, as the surrounding waters boil and can only be safely traversed with the special Drakkar ship. This biome is filled with new, formidable foes and offers powerful materials for high-tier crafting. The Ashlands update was the primary focus of development after Mistlands and serves as the penultimate challenge for players.

### Deep North

![Deep North](https://static0.gamerantimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/valheim-deep-north.jpg)

The Deep North is a frosty, high-altitude biome found at the northernmost part of the world. As of August 2025, it is still under active development and not yet fully implemented. While it currently functions as an extension of the Mountain biome in some areas, it is largely devoid of unique enemies or resources. Future updates are expected to populate this area with new content.

### Ocean

![Ocean](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcTHqXAOvj23Bhyv1-3sr7I22FGyvv2uTYy2OA&s)

The Ocean biome covers the vast bodies of water between landmasses. While it contains few resources, traversing it is essential for reaching new biomes. The main threats found in the Ocean are the dangerous Sea Serpents and the occasional Leviathans, which are large, resource-rich creatures. Sailing provides the only safe way to travel, as swimming quickly drains stamina and leads to drowning.
